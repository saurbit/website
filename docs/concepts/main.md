# 🦖✨ Saurbit

Saurbit is a constellation of modular, reusable backend packages built to roam effortlessly across Node.js, Bun, Deno, and Cloudflare Workers.

## Ancient Power, Modern Orbit

Think of it as a cosmic dinosaur: ancient-strength foundations fused with orbital‑grade flexibility. 
Each module snaps together like fossil plates forming a mighty creature, giving you a scalable, portable, and future‑proof backend ecosystem that evolves with your project. 

Packages are crafted to seamlessly integrate with others, letting you mix and match components to suit your project's unique needs while ensuring compatibility across different runtime environments.
